# 企业合规自查

> Business Compliance Self-Check Tool

> **English:** A free online tool to check your business compliance across multiple industries. Select your industries, check which licenses you already have, and get a risk assessment report with penalty warnings. 8 industries included: AI/Large Model, Medical Devices, Biotech, New Energy, Semiconductors, F&B, E-commerce, Software/IT.

> **中文：** 一个免费在线的企业合规自查工具。选行业、勾证照、出风险报告。覆盖AI/大模型、医疗器械、生物医药、新能源、半导体、餐饮零售、电商、软件IT 8个行业。

## 怎么用？

1. 打开 https://jonhnsonzz.github.io/compliance-check/
2. **选行业**：支持多选，选你涉及的行业
3. **逐项自查**：系统列出该行业必备和建议的证照，勾选已拥有的
4. **出报告**：自动评估合规风险，标注缺失项和处罚风险
